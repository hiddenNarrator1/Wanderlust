## Classes
- The Expert has had their 3rd level ability changed to **Specialty**, which gives in-combat options for a select number of skills.
- The Priest's 5th level ability has been changed to offer explicit options outside of passive miracles.
- The Mage now gains 2 spells of any Power level on levels 3 and 5.

## Mana Regeneration
Mana can now be restored at a rate of the character's Level per point of Strain.
