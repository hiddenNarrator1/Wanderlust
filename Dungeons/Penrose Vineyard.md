### People
Fred Royal: Tall and slender, well-defined muscle. Soft-spoken and a pretty ok boss. He doesn't know much about what happened and has the employees do the talking. Owner of Penrose.

Grace Amherst: Human woman, stocky and casual.
- Incident happened 4 days ago.
- Attacked near a garage with some other people. Had phosphorous in a container and threw it, where it suddenly glowed and ran off. The silhouette was seen briefly; tall, lanky, sharp claws, covered in something wriggling.
- The thing ran off to the pasture nearby.
- Pretty upset about the whole thing and may be willing to help fight.

Poppy Imwood: Nisrab, overseer for production.
- Things have been going terribly as the crops keep getting eaten and infected with some sort of aphid. Might be the vineyard next door, called Telaway Vineyard.
- Mentions a general mart not too far away, might have some clues.

Mr. Overland
- High and mighty dwarf. 
- Saw the thing on his property and blasted it. Once it ran off, he didn't care to go after it. 
- Found a little nest of wriggling things on his property, burned it all. Found a makeshift sword.
