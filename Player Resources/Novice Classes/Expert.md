### Stats
**Health**: d6 (Starting 6)  
**Mana**: 0  
**Attribute Bonuses**: +1 Any, +1 PER  
**Skill Bonuses**: Fix 1, Deduce 1, Notice 1, Rank 2 in 2 non-combat skills of your choice  
**Saving Throws**: Boost to any two of your choice.

### Abilities
**Savant (Level 1)**: Once per scene, you may reroll a failed skill check.  
**Specialties (Level 3)**: Choose three skills and mark them as your specialties. Whenever you roll with these skills, you roll with 2d8 instead of 2d6. Additionally, the chosen skills have special effects in combat when rolled, with the DC at the DM's discretion. These rolls take 3s to perform.

| Skill    | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Connect  | You had someone on the inside. A random target capable of communicating with you is actually a turncoat and rolls morale. On a failure, they retreat. On a success, they are a turncoat and help for only this battle. |
| Deduce   | You read a target like a book. On a success, the DM must tell you what the target's next most-likely action is.                                                                                                        |
| Exert    | You are a physical apex of an athlete. Success allows you to use Second Wind as part of the skill check.                                                                                                               |
| Fix      | You know a weapon inside and out. A weapon in your hands either cannot miss or cannot be used for the scene on a success.                                                                                              |
| Notice   | There's always a weak spot. Success reduces a target's armor to 0 for 1d3 rounds. This can only be done once per battle.                                                                                               |
| Perform  | Give a little panache! On a success, your flourishes inspire your team and boost their movement by 5 ft/s                                                                                                              |
