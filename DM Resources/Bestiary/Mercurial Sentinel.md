### Properties
The mercurial sentinel naturally hoards sentimental items from its victims, often making a maze out of the place they're meant to guard. Items with particularly strong value, sentimental or in wealth, become psychically linked to the sentinel, sometimes by their own volition. Touching the objects alerts them to the toucher's location.
