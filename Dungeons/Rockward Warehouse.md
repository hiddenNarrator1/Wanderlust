### POI: Warehouses
1. Located off the freeway, 6 in total.
2. Place is crawling with thugs, several of which have vehicles.
3. Crates have several markings on them. The ones with a stamp of the county upside-down are the ones with items. (There's ones with a red dragon, a ship, fragile, etc.) There are **4 crates in total**.
4. Of the crates, two can be done by 1 person, the other two need two people. Unless you crack them open, maybe...
5. There are security cameras covering the front and the main interior by 2 angles each.
6. Two MASSIVE people are overseeing the operation and making sure everyone's doing their job.
