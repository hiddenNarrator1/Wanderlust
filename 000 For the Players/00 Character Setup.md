	2. Physical (STR, END)
	3. Reflexive (AGI, PER)
3. Class Specific
	1. Grit and Guard: HP split into two. Grit adds END, Guard adds AGI. Guard is restored after fights or during with Strain.
	2. Mana for Spellcasters

- Attacks
- Skills
- Armor
- Saves
